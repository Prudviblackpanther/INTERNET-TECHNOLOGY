Theoram:Every prime number (greater than 2) modulo 4 can be written in the form4n + 1 or 4n + 3.

Proof:Any number p when divided by 4 leaves remainder 0, 1, 2 or 3. Therefore, the numberhas the form 4n, 4n + 1, 4n + 2 or 4n + 3.Since4n and4n+2areeven,so these can not be a form of a prime number which is anoddnumber (except 2). This proves the theorem.
